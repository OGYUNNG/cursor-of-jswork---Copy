Who We Are

Frosst Bank is a full-service financial institution committed to providing exceptional banking services to our customers. Founded in 1985, we've grown from a single branch to over 200 locations nationwide while maintaining our community-focused values.

As a member FDIC bank, we offer the security and stability you expect, combined with innovative financial solutions tailored to your needs.

Our History

1985

Founded in New York

FrosstBank opened its first branch in downtown Manhattan with 12 employees.

1995

Expanded to 25 Branches

We grew to serve the entire tri-state area with personal and business banking services.

2005

Launched Online Banking

Pioneered digital banking solutions with our first online banking platform.

2015

Reached $10B in Assets

Achieved significant growth while maintaining our commitment to customer service.

2023

Nationwide Presence

Community Involvement

At FrosstBank, we believe in giving back to the communities we serve. Through our foundation, we support:

  • Financial literacy programs in schools
  • Small business development initiatives
  • Affordable housing projects
  • Local food banks and shelters

In 2022 alone, we contributed over $5 million to community organizations and our employees volunteered more than 10,000 hours.
