Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our banking services

You can open a new account in three ways:

  1. Visit any of our branches with your identification documents
  2. Apply online through our website (takes about 10 minutes)
  3. Call our customer service at 1-800-FROSST-BANK

You'll need a valid government-issued ID, Social Security number, and initial deposit.

Our standard checking account requires a $25 minimum daily balance to avoid monthly fees. However, we offer several account types:

  • Basic Checking: $25 minimum balance
  • Premium Checking: $1,000 minimum balance or $5,000 in combined deposits
  • Student Checking: No minimum balance (for students under 25)

Enrolling in online banking is simple:

  1. Go to our website and click "Enroll in Online Banking"
  2. Enter your account number and personal information
  3. Create your username and password
  4. Set up security questions and verification method
  5. Accept the terms and conditions

You'll receive a confirmation email and can begin banking online immediately.

If your debit or credit card is lost or stolen, take these steps immediately:

  1. Call our 24/7 customer service at 1-800-FROSST-LOST to report it
  2. Log in to online banking to freeze the card (if possible)
  3. Monitor your account for unauthorized transactions
  4. We'll cancel your current card and issue a new one within 3-5 business days

You're protected by our $0 Liability Guarantee for unauthorized transactions.

To apply for a personal loan, you'll typically need:

  • Government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport)
  • Proof of income (recent pay stubs, tax returns, or bank statements)
  • Proof of address (utility bill or lease agreement)
  • Social Security number
  • Information about your employment

The exact requirements may vary based on your credit history and loan amount.

You can activate your new FrosstBank debit card using any of these methods:

  1. Online: Log in to your online banking account and follow the card activation prompts
  2. Mobile App: Use the card activation feature in our mobile banking app
  3. Phone: Call the activation number on the sticker on your card (available 24/7)
  4. ATM: Make a withdrawal or balance inquiry at any Frost Bank ATM

You'll need to create a PIN during activation for ATM use.
